How To

Menu

  • Base volume = 100%, so be aware of high volume.
  • + and - buttons changes the volume of the Groove Box.
  • Pause All audio playing on the Groove Box.

Drum Machine

  • Click / Touch one of the buttons.
  • Press the Key on the sound you want on your keyboard.

Sequencer

  • Add steps to the sequencer by clicking / touching the boxes.
  • To play the sequence press Play.
  • The steps marked green will play a sound.

Loops

  • To play a loop click / touch the box.
  • When green it is playing.
  • To repeat the sound press the Repeat button it will turn green.
  • To reset a loop to the start press Reset.

Backing Tracks

  • Press a box to play, it turns green to show its active.
